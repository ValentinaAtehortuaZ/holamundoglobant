Harry James Potter

He is a half-blood wizard and the only son of James and Lily Potter. He is the only person known to have survived Avada Kedavra's curse, doing so twice. Both times the curse was cast by Lord Voldemort.He is selected into Gryffindor House.

Hermione Jean Granger

She is a Muggle-born witch. Mr. and Mrs. Granger are dentists in the muggle world. At eleven years old she discovered her magical gift and she was accepted into Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Hermione began her magical studies on September 1, 1991, shortly before her twelfth birthday, and was selected for Gryffindor House. She possesses a brilliant academic mind, and proved to be a gifted student.

Ronald Bilius Weasley

He is a pureblood wizard, the sixth and youngest son of Arthur and Molly Weasley. He was also the younger brother of Bill, Charlie, Percy, Fred, and George, and the older brother of Ginny. Ron and his brothers and sister lived in the Burrow, outside Ottery St. Catchpole.He is selected into Gryffindor House.
